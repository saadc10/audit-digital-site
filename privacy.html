Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 March 2026

Who we are

Audit Digital is a trading name of SCHB Services Ltd (company no. 14440073), registered at 75 Lower Cippenham Lane, Slough, SL1 5DL. When we say "we", "us", or "our" on this page, that's who we mean.

What we collect and why

We collect personal information in two ways:

Contact form

When you submit our contact form, we collect your name, email address, firm name, and any message you include. This is processed by Formspree (our form handling provider) and forwarded to our email inbox. We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry.

Booking a consultation

When you book a call through Calendly, they collect your name, email address, and any information you provide in the booking form. Calendly processes this data under their own privacy policy. We receive your booking details so we can prepare for and conduct the call.

How we use your information

We use the information you give us to respond to enquiries, conduct consultations, and deliver our services if you choose to work with us. We do not send marketing emails unless you've specifically asked us to. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Third-party services

Our website uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

Formspree (form submissions), Calendly (appointment booking), Google Workspace (email), and GitHub Pages (website hosting). Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

How long we keep your data

We keep enquiry and client data for as long as it's needed to deliver our services and meet our legal obligations (typically six years for business records under UK law). If you ask us to delete your data, we will do so promptly unless we're legally required to retain it.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You can also object to how we process your data or request that we restrict its use. To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@auditdigital.co.uk.

Cookies

Our website does not use cookies for tracking or analytics. Third-party services embedded on our site (Calendly) may set their own cookies when you interact with them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how we handle your data, email us at hello@auditdigital.co.uk.
